With over six summers of working as a camp counselor, I can confidently say that I am one of the best in the business. Handling parents and their children with great poise is my secret talent. Building a relationship with each camper is something I look forward to doing every summer.

Although I've only worked for one city, the City of Milpitas, I have made so many connections and learned numerous skills that I could use in my future occupation. The experiences I have gained with the City of Milpitas are definitely ones that will stick with me for a long time. I first began my adventure by actually attending camp as a camper, then becoming a Counselor in Training at the age of 12 years old. After I turned 16, I applied to work as a staff member. After my first year, the city promoted me to be the counselor in training director for a summer. That experience helped me grow as a person. I gained a better work ethic and learned how to work under pressure.

Originally, I wanted a future job in the recreation department. With my experience and connections, I strongly believe I would be an amazing candidate to run the department in the future.
